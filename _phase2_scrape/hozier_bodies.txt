=== COMMENT 12 ===
Work Song. It’s such a romantic, sweet piece of music, and the lines are so beautiful:

*“i was three days on a drunken sin / i woke with her walls around me / nothing in her room but an empty crib”*

*“cause my baby’s sweet as can be / she gives me toothaches just from kissing me”*  

*“boys working on empty / is that the kind of way to face the burning heat? / i just think about my baby / i’m so full of love i could barely eat”*

Each time I go back and listen I keep finding more hidden gems. It’s been at the top of my Spotify most played songs for yeeeears and I will never grow tired of it, ever. There’s so many different ways to interpret it. 

My favorite theory is that it’s written from the perspective of a slave. Hozier’s always had a lot of spiritual/blues influence in his work and it just makes certain lines in the song make much more sense in retrospect.
