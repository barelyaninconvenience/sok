Paste 2-3 paragraphs of your own writing that represent how you sound when
writing professionally. This is NOT a resume or cover letter — it's a sample

Good candidates:
- A substantive LinkedIn post you wrote
- A well-composed email you sent (with PII stripped)
- A paragraph from a blog post, article, or essay of yours
- A section from a substantive work email explaining something technical

What NOT to use:
- Overly formal writing (it'll make cover letters stiff)
- Chat-speak or texts (too casual)
- Content you didn't write
